PRINCIPAL'S MESSAGE





Dr. Rajiv Kumar Sharma
( Principal of Wisdom International School )



Welcome to Wisdom International School. An institution which not only educates but enriches, thus empowering each young mind in the classroom. It is my pleasure to welcome you to this school, dedicated to challenging, engaging and nurturing kindergarten through tenth grade students, where our firm belief is to foster the diverse range of unique personas each of our students possesses.

Established in 2001, Wisdom Public School, at Bhilai continues to build upon a solid foundation of academic excellence combined with a warm, inclusive environment. Our students also get the opportunity to participate in before and after school enrichment activities such as Art, Chess, Martial Arts, Animation, Personality Development as well as get global exposure through the various exchange programmes that the school organizes.

It will be my personal endeavour to move forward with you as co-pactors in our quest for excellence for our children.


About Us

Wisdom International School was established in 2001 in Nehru Nagar, Bhilai, Chhattishgarh . We are working from 19 years with positive attitude.
