BUS DETAILS


School Buses:

At Wisdom Public School, for providing the most important and comfortable ride from your specified bus-stops to the School Premises, we are using our fleet of School buses. The ride in these buses to the School are ensured for both safety and comfort, by having the all important competent and friendly drivers and assistants in each of the buses.

Our Transportation crew and the vehicles ensures top-notch quality standards like:

– Certified by the Government.

– Trained and certified drivers.

– Trained assistants/helpers for emergency situations.

– “On board” first-aid facility.
