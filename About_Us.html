ABOUT US

Wisdom International School is perceived by its fascinating approach and values towards learning. We are committed to give quality education by leveraging both traditional and modern values. With an alternate procedure and methodology, our emphasis will be to develop the uniqueness of each child and take it to the highest level. Wisdom will transform into an organization that educates the criticalness of development and progression still won’t neglect the importance of customs and traditions. Our motivation is to give a space in which each student finds and understands his/her maximum capacity through global exposure without compromising on its bases of Indian society. Through our institution we develop all around advancement of characteristics that will cultivate academics, understanding, social, enthusiastic improvement and imaginative aptitudes.

OUR VISIONS

The vision of the School is to help each child become a leader – to be able to lead his or her own life, to be a strong example for others, to live by principles, to be an influence for good.




OUR MISSIONS

Every Child should be rich with Indian Values, Academic Excellence and Global perspectives. Education at Wisdom international School aims to give each child the right foundation for a lifetime of opportunity and success, Wisdom will be synonym of globally relevant quality school education in India with comprehensive and multi – dimensional academics and cultural exploration.




About Us


Wisdom International School was established in 2001 in Nehru Nagar, Bhilai, Chhattishgarh . We are working from 19 years with positive attitude.
