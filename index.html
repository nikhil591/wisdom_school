Why Choose Us

Huge School Campus

The diversification and expansion of the school through adoption of new and topically relevant courses has led to further enhancement of our infrastructure in order to fulfill our growing needs

Computer Education

Computer has taken over a major part of human activities and enabled people to have more leisure by reducing his work load. Computer Education is started from the primary classes and is one of the optional subjects in Senior Secondary Classes

Music and Dance

Training of vocal as well as instrumental music is provided according to the taste and intrest of the students from Std. I to XII 'Indian classical' and 'Western Dance' are taught in the school by experts to train, guide and help those students intrested in this form of art

Science Laboratory

The school has a well equipped laboratory of Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Computer Science,Maths and Social Studies for the students to experiment and learn because learning by doing makes concrete concept formation.







Our School Details

Admission Procedure

Please visit us with prior appointment between 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM any day from Monday to Friday and on Saturday between 9:00 AM to 11:30 AM. The admission office will give detailed information about the school, methodology, facilities, fee structure and guide you with the admission procedure followed by campus tour.

Lab Facilities

The facilities offered to students include will equipped science laboratories, well stacked library with books on all subjects, physics lab chemistry lab, highly advanced computer lab, will maintained mathematics lab, splendidly furnished language lab, craft room, sports room, yoga and meditation room, a spacious multi-purpose hall, and audio visual aids.

Sports Corner

The School holds the motto "All is well when done well"; in all curricular, co-curricular as well as extra-curricular activities. This meaningful motto inspires the staff and students to foresee and plan, to inculcate confidence aand strength, to bring our excellence and perfection so as to reach their goals by committing fully to the entrusted responsibilities.
